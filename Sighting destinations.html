Information:

In New Zealand, there are many observatories, especially universites, where you can watch the night sky.

Lake Tekapo: In Lake Tekapo, you can see the southren lights, and you can go to the observatories on top of Mt Johns or go to the Dark Sky project building on top of Cowen's hill, which offers stargazing and astronomy tours.

Great Barrier Island: In Great Barrier Island, there are many hiking trails and many places to camp. It is also the first island in the world to be established as a Dark Sky Sanctuary.

Castle Hill: Castle Hill has low pollution which makes it easier to see the night sky clearly. There are also many hiking trails you can take and you can climb the rocks to get a better view.

Fiordland National Park: Fiordland National Park has many amazing sights, campsites, and hiking trails.

Taranaki: Because of the region's isolation from major cites and bright lights, the low light pollution and dark skies makes it a good place for stargazing.
