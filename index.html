Welcome to this guide, astronomer! In this guide about stargazing in New Zealand, you can have a memorable experience watching the night sky. This guide provides information of the best destinations, a bit of facts about stars for those who love astronomy or just curious, and also photographs of views for inspiration.

Star gazing in New Zealand:

New Zealand is one of the best places in the world for stargazing. Due it's low pollution, position in the southern hemisphere, clear skies, and high evaluation, you can get clear views of the night sky, including the Milky Way. Plus, in New Zealand, you can learn about their culture, especially about Matariki.
